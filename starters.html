Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs are hard-boiled eggs sliced in half, with the yolks scooped out, mashed up with mayo and mustard, then piped back into the whites. They're creamy, tangy, and weirdly addictive. A classic party snack that somehow disappears first from every table. Simple to make, impossible to eat just one.
